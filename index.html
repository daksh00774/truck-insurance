Frequently Asked

Questions, Answered.

Do you accept insurance?
We work with most major insurance providers. Our team will verify your benefits before your first visit so there are no surprises.
How long does a typical treatment plan last?
Most patients see meaningful improvement in 4–8 weeks. Plans are personalized based on your assessment, goals, and progress.
Will I see the same therapist every visit?
Yes. We pair you with a dedicated Doctor of Physical Therapy who manages your entire recovery journey.
Do you work with competitive athletes?
Absolutely — from youth and college to professional. Our performance center is built for athletes at every level.
How do I schedule my first appointment?
Use the booking form above or call us directly. New patient evaluations are available within 24–48 hours.
